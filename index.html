ABOUT ME

As a photographer I want my photos to imagine a bold, exciting world one in which the subject, be it a product or a person, stands out, shines. Viewers need to be transported, and my photos achieve that through careful compositions of color and tone. This is the end result of a process by which I bring the creative brief to life and make the subject part of the story.

This is the power that photography's always had for me. I recognized it when I took my first photo class, back when I was growing up in Puerto Rico. So, I apprenticed with photographers and shot for variety of publications. Eventually, to continue my education, I moved to the United States.

SERVICES

COMMERCIAL PHOTOGRAPHY

Commercial photography is a genre that encompasses photos used for a business or publication. I do commercial photography for companies that have a product or service to sell and needs content to help sell it. From website to billboards to social media campaigns, there's no limit to the potential commercial uses for photos.

STUDIO SHOOTING

Our Studio photography uses a variety of background of different genres. These will include simple monochromatic background for portrait shots and complex background that simulate outdoor scenes, such as a beach or busy street, to make it look like the shot was actually taken on location. Specialized attire and props for node_modules are frequently used.

PHOTO EDITING

The photo shoot is only the first half of creating a great image. We have skilled photo editors that can take a good raw image and use a myriad of techniques to create a stunning final photo. Post-processing can enhance what you did capture and create effects that aren't always possible in the photography studio.

EVENT PHOTOGRAPHY

Event photography is the Professional art of snapping high-quality images during a wide variety of important occasions. We ara available for your personal events like weddings or birthday parties to large public gatherings like corporate events, galas, award ceremonies, and music festivals.

HEAD SHOTS AND PORTRAITS

We take head shots and portrait pictures used for purposes such as business cards, advertisements, auditions, identification badges, website profiles, social media profiles or as other means of identification.

AD PHOTOGRAPHY

Our advertising photos are high-quality and eye-catching so that the brand's message is delivered through customers engaging with the advert. The digital age has introduced effective solutions that can leverage the innate power of photo advertising and display them to wider audience for additional attention.
